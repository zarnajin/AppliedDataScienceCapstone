Results¶

  • Using cluster 0 as an example, hotels and transport can be discovered
  • Hotels are in Ariake, Omori, Shinbashi, Otemachi, Marunouchi, Hatchobori, Aoto, Asagaya, Koenji, and Ogikubo
  • Train stations are in Etchujima, Hatanodai, Oji, Shinagawa, Machiya, Shibamata, Tabata, and Ariake
  • The only district those have in common is Ariake

Discussion¶

  • The clustering did not pick up places that were less frequent, but may be more interesting
  • For accomodations, I could also look at Airbnb and hostels
  • For transport, given the distance I could also just walk or bike

Conclusions¶

  • I learned the layout of Tokyo, Japan
  • After clustering the districts, I found the most common venues and of those districts the best to stay at is Ariake
  • Further work could be done to find other suitable places to stay
